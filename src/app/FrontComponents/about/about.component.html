About Us

N2L Academy stands for “Nudge to Learn Academy”. Many people want to learn new skills like English Speaking and they join courses by paying fee. But, the fact is only 5% people complete the course. Remaining 95% people paid the fee that means they are interested to learn but they don’t have necessary discipline to do practice. A little NUDGE shall help the people to complete the course.

N2L Academy, after a long research, came out with nudging techniques. Hence, course completion rate of N2L Academy is very high. We use gamification techniques to make learning entertaining and at the same time challenging.
